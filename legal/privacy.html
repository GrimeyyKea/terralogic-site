Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

TerraLogic operates on a strict transparency protocol. We build utilities for crews running iron and moving dirt, not for tracking personal search trends or selling profiling metrics.

1. Local Environment Storage

Our "Field Mode" theme system uses your browser's native `localStorage` cache to remember if you prefer the high-visibility dark mode interface layout. This data never leaves your device and is never synchronized with outside telemetry platforms.

2. Calculation Parameters

Data inputted into our localized utility modules (e.g., compaction percentages, trench dimensions, bank yardage conversions) is processed instantly within client-side memory. No calculation metrics are stored on external database servers or retained after the browser session closes.

3. External Infrastructure Links

This resource index may feature deep links to public state civil engineering specs or municipal design guidelines. We hold no administrative oversight over the data privacy policies running on third-party government or commercial servers.
