Correcting Defective Test Zones

If the inspector's gauge flags a failing test zone, immediately check the probe depth adjustment first. If the depth is true, the quickest field solution is to cut the lift layer thickness in half on your next pass or introduce targeted moisture modification to hit the optimal Proctor curve envelope.

Proctor Reference

Always cross-verify your field gauge readings against the calibrated standard or modified Proctor curves matching the active borrow pit source.
