Site Operations & Logistics Optimization

High-output civil earthwork operations are governed directly by equipment cycle times and target payload matching. Maximizing efficiency requires keeping mechanical assets moving continuously without hitting bottlenecks at the excavation cut or the fill pad.

Balancing your haul fleet capacity against the load execution system limits downtime and structural fuel loss across high-volume grading tracks.

Bucket Fill Factor Reference

Material Condition Hydraulic Bucket Fill Factor Operational Cycle Target
Loose Granular Sand / Gravel 95% - 100% Fast penetration, optimal bucket retention
Natural In-Situ Bank Earth 80% - 90% Standard cutting force required, clean break
Hard Blasted Rock 50% - 70% High structural void count, massive cycle resistance

Haul Performance Math

Total Haul Cycle = Load Time + Haul Time + Dump Time + Return Time
Hourly Production = (Minutes Worked per Hour / Total Cycle) × Payload Volume
