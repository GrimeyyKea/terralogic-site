Soil Swell & Volume Conversion

In heavy civil earthwork, understanding the transition between Bank (in-situ), Loose, and Compacted volumes is the difference between a profitable bid and a project delay.

The Conversion Matrix

Material properties change when excavated. A bank cubic yard (BCY) will always occupy more volume when moved into a truck (Loose Cubic Yard) and must be returned to specified density (Compacted Cubic Yard).

  • Bank (BCY): Soil in its natural state.
  • Loose (LCY): Soil after excavation, increased by a swell factor.
  • Compacted (CCY): Soil after mechanical compaction to site specifications.
